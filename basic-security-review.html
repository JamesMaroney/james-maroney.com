Sometimes people ask me what they need to do to make their site "secure".

While there is a wide world of nearly endless possibilities you can consider, the reality is that with making a handful of simple good choices, and signing up for a little discipline in development, you can be much more confident that you've at least got the main bases covered, and that someone trying to harm your users has to at least work hard for it.

I wrote this document to cover the main points I consider when building or reviewing a site. Of course, this is not an exhaustive list, but it's a good place to start!
